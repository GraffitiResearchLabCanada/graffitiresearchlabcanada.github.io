Last weekend Agent Scott did a workshop at the InDigital "Tech Your Life" weekend, teaching First Nations Youth about creative code, software art, and guerilla installations.

We went through some various GRLC projects, and ended by teaching them to program a basic drawing system in Processing.

The weekend also featured installations and a conference on digital art and media empowerment.
