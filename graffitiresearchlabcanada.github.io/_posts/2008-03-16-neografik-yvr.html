March 8th & 9th saw NeoGrafik take over Vancouver. Invited by MidForms, the NeoGrafik team hit up eight locations in the downtown core over two nights of laser tagging and projected bombing mayhem.  Maps of the locations were posted online the day-of so folks could drop by start their training.Special guests for the evenings were:RhekVirusAdam DoddNeal Nolan Complete documentation to come..........Special thanks to all the volunteers who made the events possible and to New Forms for making it happen.
