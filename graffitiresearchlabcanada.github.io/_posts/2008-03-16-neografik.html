NeoGrafik is an exploration of the intersection of graffiti, projection and interactive technologies.
Currently the NeoGrafik project is comprised of two elements: Laser Tagging (as developed by the GRL-NY) and Muralling.  These are two classic forms of traditional graffiti each with their own codes and styles and so they are in the digital form as well.
