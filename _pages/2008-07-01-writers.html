AGENTS

Graffiti Research Lab Canada was co-founded in Montreal by Alexis Laurence and NomIg.

Jesse Scott co-founded the Vancouver, Toronto, and Ottawa cells, along with the GRL Germany chapter. He currently runs this site together with Mirae Rosner, who joined in 2010.

Recent Vancouver additions include flyingoctopus and Michael Manning.

GRL Canada works closely with GRL Germany- main collaborators are Hauke Altmann and Semiramis Ceylan.
